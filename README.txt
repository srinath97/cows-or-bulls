PROBLEM: Cows or Bulls is a game where we should guess a 4 letter word. 1 bull means 1 letter of the correct word and 1 letter of the guessed word 
matches in the same place. 1 cow means 1 letter of the correct word and 1 letter of the guessed word matches in the diffrent place.

This program works in DEV-C++ version 5.8.3.
